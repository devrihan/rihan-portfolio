The Driver Drowsiness Detection project leverages computer vision and machine learning to enhance road safety by monitoring drivers for signs of fatigue and alerting them in real-time. Utilizing Python and OpenCV, this application analyzes facial landmarks and blinking patterns to accurately detect drowsiness, providing timely warnings to prevent potential accidents.
